 About Me

The person in the picture is none other than myself - the creator of this website and nerd extraordinaire. I tried to find a picture of me hunched over my laptop with a large caffeinated beverage in my hand, but my family insisted I go with this one. I'm told it looks more professional or something.

Hopefully you've noticed that this site resembles an old computer monitor. That's for two reasons - I'm old and I love computers. My first experience with a computer was playing Dig Dug on my father's old Commodore 64. In high school, I was the nerd in glasses who spent all of his part-time job money buying spare computer parts and putting together various machines. Nowadays, you can find me discussing design patterns with my friends and geeking out over my new raspberry pi project.

My other passion in life turned out to be education. My love for learning is partially what draws me to technology and development. With major advancements happening so frequently it's impossible to run out of things to learn. It also drew me to teaching - as much as I enjoy learning, I love helping those around me grow into their full potential.

Teaching English as a foreign language gave me many great years and memories. Not only was I able to meet wonderful people from all around the world and learn about their cultures, I got the opportunity to live abroad in Japan for a total of six years. I was able to make some lifelong friends and gain conversational fluency.


I'm not sure where the future will take me, but I'm eager to meet any challenges head on. If you've read this far, I'd like to say thank you. I'd also like to encourage you to click the e-mail button and send me a message. I'd love to hear about exciting new opportunities or just talk shop, so don't be shy!

Have a wonderful day,

Rich
