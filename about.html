 About Me

The person in the picture is none other than myself - the creator of this website and nerd extraordinaire. I tried to find a picture of me hunched over my laptop with a large caffeinated beverage in my hand, but my family insisted I go with this one. I'm told it looks more professional or something.

Hopefully you've noticed that this site resembles an old computer monitor. That's for two reasons - I'm old and I love computers. My first experience with a computer was playing Dig Dug on my father's old Commodore 64. In high school, I was the nerd in glasses who spent all of his part-time job money buying spare computer parts and putting together various machines. Nowadays, you can find me discussing design patterns with my friends and geeking out over my new raspberry pi project.
